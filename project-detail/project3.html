Pengembangan Situs Web Food Mora Toba

Jan 2023 - Jun 2023

Product Designer & Data Analyst

Developing a product information system website for Food Mora Toba that displays local food products in an informative and attractive manner. This project is a collaboration between students of the Del Institute of Technology Group 05 Final Project 1 and the owner of Food Mora Toba

This website is designed not only as an information medium, but also as a means of digital promotion to support the marketing of local MSME products. Users can access complete information about Food Mora Toba, including a list of products, contacts, addresses, and other information simply by visiting the website address. To ensure that information is always up to date, this system also provides an admin panel that allows managers to update content regularly.

In its development, this project includes the stages of needs analysis, user interface design (UI/UX), and implementation using modern web technology. It is hoped that this site can increase the visibility and competitiveness of Food Mora Toba in the digital era.
